iLASH Performance

A customized implementation of LSH algorithm, together with some software optimization best-practices, have made iLash a order of magnitude faster than its closest competitor, GERMLINE. We tested both iLASH and GERMLINE on simulated data based on PAGE consortium IBD distribution with different individual counts. As seen in the figure, iLASH shows a near-linear order of runtime based on the size of the dataset, while GERMLINE grows rapidly.

Power

While faster, iLASH also manage to find more results than GERMLINE. It also shows a really high concordance with GERMLINE results. At the same time, we have shown that iLASH finds near 0% false negatives. As seen in the second figure, iLASH was able to find more result that GERMLINE based on a simulated ground truth in different datasets.

Customizability

Almost every aspect of the iLASH can be manipulated simply through its configuration file. Please refer to documentation section to learn more about iLASH configuration.
